## Description

A clean, simple, and private way to track your money. Budget Control helps you stay on top of your spending without connecting your bank accounts or creating an account.

Features:
- Track income and expenses across multiple wallets
- Set monthly budgets per category and get alerts when you are close
- Visualize spending with beautiful charts
- Plan trips and automatically tag travel expenses
- Set savings goals and track your progress
- Track subscriptions and see your monthly total
- Export your data as CSV or JSON anytime
- Full dark mode support
- No accounts, no servers, no ads — all data stays on your device
